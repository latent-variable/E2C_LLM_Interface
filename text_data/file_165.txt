english_summary: The Ministry of Commerce of the People's Republic of China announced the "Declaring Requirements for State Trading Enterprises to Export Tungsten, Antimony and Silver for the Years of 2024-2025 and the Declaring Procedures" on November 9, 2023. The announcement is aimed at strengthening the management of export of rare metals, protecting resources and the environment, and complying with the Foreign Trade Law of the People's Republic of China and the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of the Import and Export of Goods.
