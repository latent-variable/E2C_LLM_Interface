The list includes 66 enterprises eligible for bidding on fresh or dried licorice, 23 enterprises eligible for bidding on licorice juice and extract, and 26 enterprises eligible for bidding on glycyrrhizic acid powder, glycyrrhizic acid salts, glycyrrhizic acid and its derivatives, and other glycyrrhizic acid.
