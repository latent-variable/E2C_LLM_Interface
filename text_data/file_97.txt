english_summary: The Ministry of Commerce of the People's Republic of China has published the "Measures for the Administration of the Circulation of Refined Oil Products (Draft for Public Consultation)" to strengthen the management of refined oil products, regulate business behavior, and maintain domestic circulation order. The public consultation period lasts until July 11, 2023, and the public can submit their opinions through various ways such as online submission on the Ministry of Justice of the People's Republic of China's website (www.moj.gov.cn, www.chinalaw.gov.cn) or the Ministry of Commerce's website (www.mofcom.gov.cn), email (ciie_zs@mofcom.gov.cn), fax (010-85093284), or letter (Department of Consumption Promotion, Ministry of Commerce, No. 2, East Chang'an Street, Beijing, China, Postal Code: 100731) by indicating the specific public consultation title in the subject line of the e-mail, on the first page of the fax, and on the envelope.
